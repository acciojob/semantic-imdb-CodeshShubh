PK (2014) is an Indian satirical science fiction film directed by Rajkumar Hirani. The movie follows an alien named PK (played by Aamir Khan), who arrives on Earth and struggles to understand human behavior, culture, and religion. He questions the belief systems and superstitions that people follow, often challenging societal norms and exposing hypocrisies. PK befriends a journalist, Jaggu (played by Anushka Sharma), who helps him navigate the complexities of Earth. Through PK's innocent perspective, the film explores themes of faith, religion, and human nature. The movie ultimately highlights the importance of love, understanding, and questioning blind beliefs. The film was a critical and commercial success and sparked discussions about religious intolerance and societal norms.
Aamir Khan (PK): Aamir Khan delivers a memorable performance as the innocent and curious alien, PK. Known for his versatility and commitment to his roles, Aamir brings a unique blend of humor and thoughtfulness to the character. His portrayal of an outsider questioning human beliefs and behaviors is both entertaining and thought-provoking.
